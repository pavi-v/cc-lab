About KG Hospital

KG Hospital is a recognized Designated Blood Bank for nearly 200 hospitals located in Coimbatore and the adjoining areas.
The Hospital is also a beneficiary of the Prime Minister's Relief Fund and Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Relief Fund for providing medical care to the poorer sections of the society.
KG Hospital is recognized under the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme for treating people below the poverty line.
The Hospital has received quality accreditation from the NABH(National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers) of Quality Council of India, New Delhi.
It is the first hospital in this part of the country to receive the "Five Star Award" from the Quality Circle Forum of India (QCFI) for practicing the "5S" concept.
